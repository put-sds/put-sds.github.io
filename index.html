Software Development Studio

Learn software engineering pracitices while taking part in a software project!

Welcome!

Software Development Studio is an initiative at the Poznan University of Technology, which gives students an opportunity to develop projects for real customers. Each project is developed by the team consisting of the students from the SE Master degree course, who are playing the project manager analyst, architect, and the third-year students who are the software developers (the developed project is also a part of their B. Sc. thesis). More information you can find here.
